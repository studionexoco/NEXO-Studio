Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 3, 2026

1. Introduction

NEXO Studio ("NEXO", "we", "our", or "us") is a digital product and UX design studio committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your information is handled.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

NEXO Studio operates primarily as a portfolio and informational website. We collect only limited personal information that you voluntarily provide.

When you use our contact form, we may collect:

  • Government identification numbers
  • Sensitive personal data

Depending on your interaction with the website, basic technical data may be collected automatically, such as:

This data is used strictly for improving website performance and user experience.

The information collected is used solely for legitimate business purposes, including:

If you contact NEXO Studio via the website contact form, your email address and message will be used exclusively to respond to your inquiry.
