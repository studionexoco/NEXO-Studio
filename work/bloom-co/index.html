Bloom & Co.

Floral Commerce Experience

Designing a calm, premium mobile shopping journey for meaningful floral gifting.

Platform iOS / Android
Domain Floral E-commerce · Gifting
Role Product Designer (UX + UI)

Bloom & Co is a floral commerce mobile experience focused on emotional gifting moments.

The product simplifies flower discovery, customization, checkout, and delivery tracking into a soft, frictionless journey.

User Journey

Browse → Product → Customize → Cart → Checkout → Payment → Tracking → Orders

Product Discovery

The browsing experience emphasizes bouquet imagery and emotional categories such as occasions and sentiments.

Products appear as soft rounded cards with neutral backgrounds to highlight flowers. Users can quickly understand arrangement type, tone, and pricing without cognitive load.

Product Details

The product page focuses on visual clarity and emotional storytelling.

Size selection and add-ons are presented as clear selectable chips instead of dropdowns.

Photography and description reinforce gifting intent while maintaining calm visual hierarchy.

Cart & Checkout

Cart and checkout reduce friction through progressive disclosure.

Delivery date, time, and payment are grouped into a single calm flow.

Pricing clarity and minimal steps support quick decision completion for gifting moments.

Payment Methods

Payment selection uses recognizable financial visuals and minimal typography. Large tap targets and simple hierarchy improve speed and confidence during purchase.

Order Tracking

Order tracking provides clear delivery stages with timestamp feedback. Progress indicators reduce anxiety and reinforce reliability during fulfillment.

Order History & Profile

Order history presents past purchases as visual cards for quick recall. Users can revisit gifting moments and reorder easily.

The profile area centralizes account, orders, addresses, and support. Soft icons and spacing maintain brand calmness across utility screens.

Final Showcase

Bloom & Co transforms floral shopping into a calm, emotionally resonant gifting experience.

Design System

Built on a foundation of soft elevation, rounded cards, image-first hierarchy, calm spacing, and minimal borders.
